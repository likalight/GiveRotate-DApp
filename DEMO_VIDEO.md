## What the demo covers

- Minting XSGD to donor wallets via Etherscan
- Registering 4 MCCY-registered IPC charities across 2 cause pools
- Setting pool-level perks for Animal Welfare and Social Rehabilitation
- Donor flow — all 3 donors donating, NFT receipts minting, perks unlocking
- SingPass MyInfo oracle — generating IRAS 250% tax deduction PDFs
- Admin triggering payout — 175 XSGD split equally between SPCA Singapore and Animal Lovers League
- ROSCA rotation advancing automatically to the Social Rehabilitation pool
- Charity view — verifying on-chain payout receipt
